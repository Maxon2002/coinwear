Refund Policy
1. Eligibility
To be eligible for a refund, you must meet the following criteria:
- The item(s) must be in their original condition, unused, and with all tags or labels intact.
- The refund request must be made within 7 days from the date of delivery.
- The reason for the refund must fall within the scope of our refund policy (e.g., defective or damaged items).
2. Refund Process
2.1 Return Authorization: To initiate a refund, please contact our customer support team to obtain a return authorization. We may request additional information or evidence to support your refund request.
2.2 Return Shipping: You are responsible for shipping the item(s) back to us in their original packaging. We recommend using a trackable shipping method to ensure the item(s) reach us safely.
2.3 Refund Approval: Once we receive the returned item(s) and inspect their condition, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, the refund will be processed to your original method of payment within a reasonable timeframe.
3. Exceptions
The following exceptions apply to our refund policy:
- Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a defective item.
4. Contact Us
If you have any questions or need further assistance regarding our refund policy, please contact our customer support team using the provided contact information.
