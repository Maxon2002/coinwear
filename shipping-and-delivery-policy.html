Shipping and Delivery Policy
1. Estimated Delivery Time
We strive to deliver orders within 14 days from the date of purchase. Please note that this is an estimated timeframe and actual delivery times may vary depending on factors such as shipping destination and customs processing.
2. Shipping Costs
We are pleased to offer free shipping on all orders. You won't have to worry about any additional shipping charges. Enjoy the convenience of browsing our products and having them delivered to your doorstep without any cost.
3. Order Processing Time
Once an order is placed, it will typically take 1-2 business days to process before shipment. During peak seasons or promotional periods, processing times may be slightly longer. We appreciate your patience.
4. Tracking Information
Once your order has been shipped, we will provide you with a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to monitor the progress of your shipment and estimated delivery date.
5. International Shipping
For international orders, please note that additional customs fees, duties, or taxes may apply upon arrival in the destination country. These charges are the responsibility of the customer and will be assessed and collected by the customs authority of the respective country.
6. Address Accuracy
It is the customer's responsibility to provide accurate and complete shipping information. We cannot be held responsible for delays or non-delivery of orders due to incorrect or incomplete address details provided by the customer.
7. Delivery Delays
While we make every effort to ensure timely delivery, unforeseen circumstances such as weather conditions, customs delays, or other logistical issues may occasionally result in delays beyond our control. We appreciate your understanding in such situations.
